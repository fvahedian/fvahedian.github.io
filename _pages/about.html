I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow with the Graph Exploration and Mining at Scale (GEMS) at the University of Michigan – Ann Arbor under the supervision of Dr. Danai Koutra. Before that, I was a postdoctoral research fellow with Complex Networks (CoNe) Lab at the University of Notre Dame. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the DePaul University - Chicago, IL focusing on heterogenous network mining and hybrid recommendation under the supervision of Dr. Robin Burke. 

Research Interest:

My research is focused on applied machine learning, network/graph mining and recommender systems. I am interested in investigating complex systems problems and develop multi-relational machine learning methods to solve these problems.
